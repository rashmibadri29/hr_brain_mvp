# PTO And Leave Policy

Full-time employees accrue PTO each pay period. PTO requests should be submitted in the HRIS at least five business days before the requested dates.

Sick leave is available according to the employee's work location and employment type. Employees should not share medical details in Slack or email.

Parental leave questions, FMLA questions, disability accommodation requests, and medical leave requests must be escalated to the HR benefits owner.

Policy exceptions require HR operations review and manager approval.

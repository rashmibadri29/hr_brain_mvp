New Hire Onboarding Checklist

Collect employee name, start date, manager, role, employment type, and work location.

Before the first day, HR confirms signed offer paperwork, payroll profile, benefits eligibility, and tax forms. IT prepares laptop, email, identity provider access, and required SaaS accounts.

On the first day, the manager hosts role orientation, confirms team introductions, and reviews the 30 day onboarding plan.

Escalate to HR operations if employment type, work location, or manager assignment is missing.
